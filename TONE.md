# Tone & Voice — Luminary's Copy Bible

Every text moment is a chance to feel like Luminary. This document is the rule set.

## Voice north star

Luminary speaks like a **thoughtful friend who reads a lot and notices things.** Calm, warm, observant, lightly literary. Never preachy, never therapeutic-jargon-y, never gamified-sycophantic ("Amazing! 🎉").

We are NOT:
- Apple Health (clinical, distant)
- Calm/Headspace (hushed, woo)
- Duolingo (loud, manipulative streak shaming)
- Strava (gym-bro performance)

We ARE:
- The voice of a journal that writes back, occasionally.
- Editorial, like a thoughtful magazine pull-quote.
- Direct enough to be useful, soft enough to be safe.

## The five voice rules

1. **Lead with observation, not instruction.** "You've listened to Sonder three nights in a row." > "Try a new artist!"
2. **Lower-case the small things.** Sentence case for body, headlines, buttons. Title Case feels corporate.
3. **One italic per screen, max.** Used for emphasis on a single word. *really*, *seen*, *now*. Save it.
4. **Questions over commands.** "How are you really feeling?" beats "Log your mood." We invite, we don't task-master.
5. **No exclamation points** unless we're imitating a user. Even "Great job!" should be retired.

## The vocabulary

| ✅ Use | ❌ Avoid |
|---|---|
| capture, log, note | track (clinical) |
| check-in, ritual, evening | session, log entry |
| notice, observe, see | analyze, detect |
| consistency, rhythm | streak (when used punitively) |
| your evening, tonight | end of day |
| mood, feeling, resonance | emotional state |
| money, spending | budget allocation |
| quiet, settle, wind down | relax, unwind |

## Reusable phrases

These are pre-approved. Reach for them.

- **Greeting (evening, returning):** "Welcome back."
- **Greeting (morning):** "Good morning. How did last night settle?"
- **Habit complete:** "Captured." (no celebration)
- **Streak band achieved:** "5 of 7 this week. That counts."
- **Streak band missed:** "Tomorrow's a clean slate."
- **Spotify mood-confirm primary:** "That's about right."
- **Spotify mood-confirm rejection:** "Not quite right." (NOT "Adjust" — must be conversational)
- **Empty journal state:** "Nothing here yet. Tonight could be the first one."
- **Empty habits state:** "Pick something small. Three is enough."
- **Locked module CTA:** "Open this when you're ready."
- **End of ritual:** "See you tomorrow evening."

## The Friend Card voice

The daily editorial card on Home, generated each evening for next morning. Templates pre-MVP, LLM post-MVP.

Length: 1–2 sentences. Always declarative. Never asks a question (the Mood check is the question).

Examples:
- "You listened to Sonder three days in a row. That's usually a thinking-through-something week for you."
- "Three habits, three days. Quiet but compounding."
- "Last night's mood was the highest it's been since April 12. Nothing to do with it — just a thing that's true."
- "You skipped Wednesday. The week is still yours."

## Notification copy

| Moment | Copy |
|---|---|
| Evening ritual reminder | "Time to wind down. Tonight's check-in is waiting." |
| Streak band hit | "5 of 7 this week. The shape is holding." |
| Spotify reconnect needed | "Spotify needs a quick reconnect. We'll wait." |
| First-time onboarding nudge | "Your first evening is one tap away." |
| Weekly review ready (Sunday) | "The week, in review. Take a moment." |

## Error & failure copy

Errors get the same voice. They are not opportunities for sass.

- Network error: "We can't reach the server right now. Your data is safe — we'll sync when we're back."
- Spotify token expired: "Spotify needs you to sign in again. One tap."
- Validation: lower-case, kind. "We need an email here." not "Email is required."

## What to do when a screen doesn't have copy yet

Write a placeholder in this voice. `// TODO: tone-pass` flags it for review. Never ship lorem ipsum or default Material/iOS strings.
